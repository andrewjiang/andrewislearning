## Architecture decisions made today

These shaped the whole repo, not just Day 1. Capturing here because they came out of the Day 1 brainstorm:

1. **Per-day subfolders inside every stage** (`raw/day_01/`, `edits/day_01/`, etc.). Day number is the join key across `raw/`, `transcripts/`, `edits/`, `published/`, `series/days/`, `plugins/day-NN-*/`. Symmetry > flat naming. Enables `./ship.sh day_05`-style commands later.
2. **Shot-type prefix in raw filenames** (`selfie_*`, `screen_*`, `broll_*`). Lets `cut.py` (Day 4) tell A-roll from B-roll without watching the file.
3. **The day SHIPS the plugin.** Day 1's deliverable isn't just a video — it's `day-01-series-planner` as an installable Claude Code plugin. Subsequent days follow the same pattern: each day's recipe gets bottled.
4. **Manual on-screen cards for Shot 3** are intentional. Remotion arrives Day 8. The fact that Day 1 was hand-rolled IS part of the story.

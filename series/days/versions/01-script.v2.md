## Why this was replaced

- No series-format opener. "Day 1 of teaching an AI to be my video editor" was missing — important for series identity from episode one (consistent across all 30, lets later viewers find Day 1 and recognize the format).
- "I've shipped AI demos that got millions of views" was a vague abstract credential. The 1M views on X (concrete, story-embedded) is a sharper specific.
- "I'm spending the next thirty days" was confident-but-detached. "Challenging myself" is more relatable for an audience of beginners who feel intimidated by editing — signals "I don't already know how to do this either."
- "A plugin you can install in your own Claude" risked confusing the entrepreneur half of the audience. "Free plugin" + DM with install instructions is cleaner.
- Some throat-clearing in the body: "I haven't edited anything yet" → "no edits yet"; "the next thirty days" → "30 days".

## Why this was replaced

- **No b-roll on the cold open.** The "nearly a million views" + "two thousand comments" claims sat as raw verbal stats. Adding the X video clip and IG repost as b-roll converts those from claims into receipts.
- **Missing thesis bridge.** "I got out-edited" → "So I'm challenging myself" had no logical bridge. The audience didn't see *why* the answer was "teach an AI" rather than "learn editing." v3.2 inserts the contrarian thesis explicitly: *"instead of becoming an editor, I'm gonna teach an AI to be one."*
- **"Day 1, no edits yet."** Too clever — "edits" is ambiguous, and the line is doing inside-baseball work that doesn't land in 2 seconds. Replaced with *"Step one wasn't recording. It was opening Claude."*
- **Three phases, not four.** The "three phases" framing implied sequential weeks. Replaced with four cards ending on *"Every day."* — breaks the phase reading and surfaces the daily-compounding thesis.
- **Cards silent.** v3.1 had cards as visual punctuation while you went silent. v3.2 has you narrate the cards (1.5s/each, 1:1 sync with the visual) so there's no audio gap.
- **"Free plugin"** lands as jargon for the entrepreneur audience. v3.2 calls it *"a free guide"* — accessible framing of the same deliverable.
- **Generic CTA.** "Comment 'DAY1' and I'll DM you" → "Follow and comment 'DAY1' and I'll DM you" — folds the follow into the value transaction (algo-safe, useful for Day 1's follower base).

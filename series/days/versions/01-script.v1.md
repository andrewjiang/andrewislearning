## Why this was replaced

- Cold open was too generic ("got more views than mine"). The real story — 1M views on X, IG repost giving away the repo link, 2K comments — is far more specific and didn't make it into v1.
- Three credentials stacked in the cold open ("serial entrepreneur," "$60M raised," "AI projects with millions of views"). Research on Gen Z / short-form trust signals shows credential dumps trigger status distance with beginner audiences.
- Four cards in 6 seconds was rushed.
- "Follow if you want…" is a generic CTA that the 2026 IG algorithm penalizes as engagement bait.
- Frame leaned victim ("got beat at my own content") instead of ownership.
